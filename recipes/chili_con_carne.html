Chili Con Carne

Description:

This hearty and flavorful chili con carne is packed with tender beef, beans, and a rich, spiced tomato sauce. Perfect for a cozy meal!

Ingredients:

  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500 g minced beef (or ground beef)
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 30 g tomato purée
  • 400 g canned kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 250 ml beef stock (or water)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili powder
  • Smoked paprika

Steps:

  1. Sauté the aromatics. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until soft. Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  2. Brown the beef. Add the minced beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add the vegetables and spices. Stir in the chopped bell pepper, tomato purée, cumin, paprika, chili powder, cinnamon, and cayenne. Cook for 2 minutes to release the flavors.
  4. Simmer the chili. Pour in the chopped tomatoes and beef stock. Bring to a simmer, then lower the heat and cook uncovered for 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the beans. Stir in the kidney beans and cook for another 10 minutes. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve & enjoy. Serve hot over rice or with bread. Garnish with fresh coriander and a dollop of sour cream if desired.
