Pancakes

Description:

These light and fluffy pancakes are perfect for breakfast or brunch! Serve them with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 10 g baking powder
  • 5 g sugar
  • 2 g salt
  • 250 ml milk
  • 1 egg
  • 30 g melted butter (plus extra for cooking)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract (optional)
  • Maple syrup or honey
  • Fresh fruit (bananas, berries, etc.)
  • Whipped cream or yogurt

Steps:

  1. Prepare the batter. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  2. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. Do not overmix—a few lumps are okay!
  3. Heat the pan. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter.
  4. Cook the pancakes. Pour about 60 ml (¼ cup) of batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm. Stack the pancakes and drizzle with syrup, add fruit, or any toppings you like!
